For Farmers/Croppers:

  • Maximize oilseed harvest value
  • Secure fuel needs, and keep using current machinery
  • Lower capital, expertise, and time costs through shared facility use
  • Reduce CO2 emissions and access penalty-free processing for impacted yields

For Stock-feed plants:

  • Access the protein in oilseeds, without diluting oil
  • Increased shelf life of oilseeds with field weevils in harvest cooked through processing 
  • Increase protein production for the industry as more crop producers recognize the growing need to secure fuel
  • Develop new biomedical parasite combating compounds

For Livestock producers:

All the benefits for the Stock-feed plant plus:

  • Can be fed direct to livestock during drought, without negatively impacting gut biome
  • Biscuits can be a behavioral modifier 
  • Supports the possibility of diversifying into agroforestry through oilseed trees

For Cotton Gins or Hemp/Flax Mills:

  • Increase the services and return to the grower by maximizing the value of the seed
  • Behind the meter power generation from the seed oil
  • Reduce the storage volume of the seed

About

We are a passionate and experienced group of engineers and agricultural experts who recognize that food security is dependent on fuel security, and that our national fuel security situation can be described as tenuous at best. We think that maximizing the value of a farmer’s oilseed harvest is through on-shore processing and fuel refining, developing local rural economies and communities, benefiting both the farmer and the planet.
